Our Goal

Grass Touchers are dedicated to informing the public about environmental problems. With the rise in waste and pollution more awareness is needed to create change. We hope that through our trivia and informative article, we can make steps towards having a greener future.

About our team

Grass Touchers consist of dedicated College-level students. All of us at Grass Touchers are passionate about working on spreading correct information. We hope that our efforts create a positive change in our beautiful world.
